About Me

Hello, I am Matthew Lee, a second year at UC Berkeley Studying Elecrical Engineering and Computer Sciences. I am passionate about all things hardware and software, with a current emphasis in learning more about analog design and computer architecture

I am currently taking classes on Microelectronic Devices and Circuits, learning the fundamental circuit and semiconductor device concepts needed to understand analog integrated circuits; Signals and Systems, learning the background behind CT and DT analysis alongside communication and feedback systems; Computer Architecture, learning the fundamentals of the entire computer architecture stack

Outside of school, I enjoy building guitar pedals, working on robotics, and experimenting with analog audio design.
